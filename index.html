Aunabil Chakma

I am a second-year Computer Science PhD student at the University of Arizona, working with Professors Eduardo Blanco and Mihai Surdeanu. I am broadly interested in improving large language models, including their training, reasoning capabilities, alignment, and ability to adapt to new tasks.

My recent work has focused on relation extraction, prompt optimization, and in-context learning, including methods for selecting better examples and refining prompts for few-shot tasks.
